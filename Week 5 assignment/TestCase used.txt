Testcase ID: TC_UPI_001
   Title: Successful UPI Fund Transfer
   Description: Verify that a user can successfully transfer funds using UPI.
   Pre-conditions: User is logged in and has sufficient balance.
   Steps:
     1. Navigate to the UPI fund transfer section.
     2. Enter the recipient's UPI ID.
     3. Enter the amount to be transferred.
     4. Confirm the transaction details.
     5. Authorize the transaction using OTP or biometric authentication.
   Expected Result: The fund transfer is successful, and a confirmation message is displayed.
